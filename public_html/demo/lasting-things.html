placethings

Create compelling,
lasting "things"

A geotagged image by itself doesn't mean much, and location-based status messages become "stale" quickly. Our "things" become part of the digital landscape of a place. Instead of just geotagging an image or tweet, when you place a thing you start a conversation with video, sounds, images, and text. You are adding to a place, enhancing it.
